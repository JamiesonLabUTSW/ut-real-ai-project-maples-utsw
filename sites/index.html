Partner Institutions

Six UT Medical Schools, One Platform

This project, funded by the UT REAL Health AI Pilot Program, brings together six University of Texas health institutions — from the state's largest medical centers to its newest medical school. UT Southwestern serves as the coordinating hub with a production-deployed AI grading system; partner sites are in the planning and onboarding phase.
